# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

This skill contains campaign finance information and voter data schemas. If you identify a security concern — including incorrect legal information that could cause compliance harm — please report it privately.

**Contact:** dougdevitre@gmail.com

**Response time:** Within 48 hours for legal/compliance errors. Within 7 days for other issues.

## Scope

- Incorrect contribution limits, filing deadlines, or prohibited source rules
- Broken agency links that could direct users to phishing sites
- Data schemas that could expose PII if implemented without modification
- Any content that could facilitate illegal campaign activity

## Not in Scope

- Typographical errors (open a regular issue)
- Outdated information that is clearly marked with a `last_verified` date (open a regular issue)
- Disagreements about campaign strategy (these are opinions, not vulnerabilities)
